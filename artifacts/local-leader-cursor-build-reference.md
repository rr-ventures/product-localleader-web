## BRAND & VOICE

**One-liner:** We help Australian trade businesses become the #1 tradie in their area.

**Tone:** Trustworthy, casual-confident, plain-spoken Australian. Like a smart mate who knows Google inside out. Never salesy, never jargon-heavy.

**Language rules:**
- Say "tradie" — never "trade professional" or "contractor"
- Outcomes before mechanisms ("your phone rings more" before "we optimise your GBP")
- Australian spelling (optimise, specialise, colour)
- Never use: "leverage", "synergy", "digital transformation", "solution"

## AUDIENCE (for copy targeting)

Australian trade business owners — plumbers, electricians, builders, landscapers, concreters, painters, roofers, tilers. Male-skewed, 25–55. Sole traders to small teams. ## THREE PILLARS

### Pillar 1: Top of Google Maps
**Headline:** Show up first when locals search for your trade.
**Body:** When someone searches "[your trade] near me" on their phone, Google shows 3 businesses on the map. If you're not in those top 3, you're invisible. We get you there — and keep you there — so your phone rings from people ready to hire, not tyre-kickers.
**How:** We fully optimise your Google Business Profile, build your presence across 50+ directories, and manage your Maps ranking month to month across your whole service area.

### Pillar 2: First Page of Google
**Headline:** Your business on Page 1 — not your competitors, not some directory. You.
**Body:** When locals search for your trade in your area, your business shows up first. Organic traffic that comes to you every day without spending a cent on ads.
**How:** We build SEO-optimised pages on your website targeting the exact words your customers type when they're ready to book, fix technical issues most trade websites carry, and expand to surrounding suburbs as your rankings grow.

### Pillar 3: 5-Star Review Engine
**Headline:** Build a wall of 5-star reviews — without lifting a finger.
**Body:** More reviews means more trust means more calls. Your Google profile builds a reputation that makes you the obvious choice before anyone even picks up the phone.
**How:** We set up an automated system that sends review requests after every job. Happy customers go straight to Google. Customers with a concern go to a private feedback form. Every review gets a professional response.

## FAQ SECTION

**I've been burned by marketing people before. How is this different?**
We build assets you own — your Google ranking, your Maps presence, your reviews. They don't disappear overnight. And at $500/month with no lock-in after 3 months, the risk is about as low as it gets.

**How long until I see results?**
Most businesses see movement in the first 30 days. Some get calls within 2 weeks just from the Google Maps work alone. SEO compounds — month 2 is better than month 1, and it keeps building.

**I'm too busy / don't need more work right now.**
That's the best time to build this. Word of mouth dries up. We build a pipeline that keeps your phone ringing when things slow down.

**Can't I just do this myself?**
You could. But most tradies try for a week, get busy with real jobs, and it never gets done. We do this full time, every week.

**$500 a month is a lot.**
What's your average job worth? One extra job from Google more than covers it. And there's no ad spend on top.

**What's the catch with the early bird pricing?**
No catch. We're launching a new offer and giving our first clients a significant discount — $500/month instead of $2,000. You keep that rate as long as you stay. Limited spots.

**Is there a lock-in contract?**
3-month initial commitment so we have time to get real results. Month-to-month after that. You stay because it's working, not because you're trapped.

**What do I actually need to do?**
Almost nothing. Give us access to your Google Business Profile and website, and a list of your services and service area. We handle everything else.

**Do you work with my type of trade?**
We work with plumbers, electricians, builders, painters, landscapers, concreters, roofers, tilers — any trade business that serves a local area and wants more local customers finding them on Google.

## DESIGN DIRECTION

- Clean, light, professional. Typography-led over heavy imagery.
- Left-aligned hero preferred.
- Mobile-first (tradies browse on their phones).
- Light backgrounds, high contrast.
- Whitespace that feels confident, not empty.
- Fast page load — no heavy assets.
- **Avoid:** Dark/moody backgrounds, neon, gradients, startup/tech-bro vibes, excessive animations.
