Before Making Your Ads Do This: 
Step 1 = Make sure you underlying offer is validated and well researched. 
Step 2 = Can the offer be improved? (Guarantee, risk reversal, scarcity, urgency, bonus’s, offering more in general?) 
The ads wont work unless the underlying offer and messaging being baked into the creative has alignment with what the market wants, needs and will respond to. 
Don’t just make creatives willy nilly with no clear strategy backing it up. 




The Two Invisible Forces Behind Every Winning Creative: 
1️⃣ Curiosity – The need to find out something.
2️⃣ Transformation – The desire to achieve an outcome.

Curiosity: 
Curiosity is an open loop in the brain a question that demands an answer. When people see something that suggests they might be missing out on valuable information, they feel compelled to find out what they don’t know.
This is why mystery novels sell. Why news stories hook readers. Why clickbait headlines exist. And why, in lead generation, curiosity-based ads get some of the highest engagement rates possible.
"Looking for a dentist? Call us today!" > "Are you making this mistake with your teeth?"
"Need an agent? Contact me now!" > "How much could your home be worth in today’s market?"

Transformation: 
Every product or service sells an outcome. The problem is, most businesses market themselves as if they’re selling a process and people don’t buy processes. They buy results.
A cosmetic dentist doesn’t sell teeth whitening appointments.They sell the feeling of confidence that comes from a flawless smile.
A weight loss clinic doesn’t sell workout programs.They sell the vision of seeing yourself in the mirror and feeling proud.
The key is to trigger emotional resonance. When someone sees the ad, they need to think, "That could be me." Because when people see a transformation that they deeply desire, they will take action to make it their own reality.

The Highest Success Rate Creative Frameworks: 

#1 = The “Before & After” Formula (Perfect for Med Spas, Dentists, Home Services, and More)
Transformation sells so show it. If your client provides a visual transformation, there is no reason to run anything else.
A stock photo of a generic smiling woman with the text:
"Rejuvenate Your Skin with Our Advanced Facial Treatments!"

VS
Left Side: A woman with dry, dull skin.
Right Side: The same woman, glowing and flawless after a Hydrafacial.
Overlay Text: “Dull, Dry Skin? Try Our Hydrafacial & Get an Instant Glow—50% Off Today Only!”
#2 = The “Curiosity Gap” Creative (Ideal for Real Estate, Mortgage Brokers, Finance, and Insurance)
Some businesses don’t create visual transformations, but they do answer burning questions. This is where curiosity-based creatives shine. Think about it people love knowing things others don’t.

"Thinking of Selling? Contact Us for a Free Home Valuation!" 

VS 

📢 Breaking: Home Prices Have Increased in [CITY]—Find Out What Your Home Is Worth in 60 Seconds!
#3 The “Urgency & Scarcity” Hook (For High-Ticket Services, Gyms, Beauty, and Any Business That Needs Quick Conversions)
Some ads work because they trigger a primal fear of missing out.
People hate to lose out on a good deal more than they love finding one. So if your client’s offer is limited, you should be making that the focus of the creative.
"Join Our Gym Today and Get Started on Your Fitness Journey!"
VS
🚨 Only 5 Spots Left! Get 50% Off Personal Training, This Week Only! 🚨
